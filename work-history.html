My Work History

If you would like to skip the overview and go staight to my resume, click here. Links to some of my coding projects can be found at the bottom of the page.

In 2004 at the age of 15 I was employed by Bob See as a Glassy at the Grand Hotel in Gladstone. I worked for Bob for roughly two years and gained a knowledge of hospitality and customer service.

I also worked briefly for Players nightclub (now defunct) in Gladstone, overlapping my time at the Grand Hotel an finishing up just after I graduated highschool in 2006.

After highschool, I joined the NAVY as an Marine Technician. I served for six years. During this time I was deployed to Melbourne and Sydney. I served on ships HMAS Manoora, Kanimbla and Tobruk. While in the navy I attained a cert 4 in Marine Tech. Some Of my many qualifications from this time include, welding/brazing/soldering, electrical, woodwork, machining and lathing, heavy diesel and plant mechanical and many more. I completed further study in the area of gauge and instrument calibration. I left the NAVY with an honourable discharge in late 2012. My time in the military instilled discipline in me, and pride in my work.

In 2013 after I left the military I briefly worked at the Gladstone Airport as a baggage handler for Virgin Airlines. This job was one of the most enjoyable I've ever had. The workplace had a high sense of comradery. This job helped to reinforce ethical values in me, being trusted handling customers' belongings safley while still hittings all timeframes.

Late in 2013, I was offered a job by Bechtel to work on the construction G.L.N.G natural gas terminal as a Trade's assistant. Again, I was blessed to have a high sense of comradery in this job and learnt many things in the area of electrical and safety. I was made redundant as the construction was coming to a close in late 2015

After G.L.N.G, I received an offer to work in my father's business in Bundaberg. He the owns ENZED hydraulics Bundaberg branch. I worked this job as a hydraulic fitter and eventually manager, only leaving to start my full-stack web course in Feb 2022. ENZED helped my to learn about managing staff and balancing work/customer relationships. It was always a balancing act to keep my employers, my team and my customers happy all at once! But it was a challenge that I rose to.
